# Rescued by the Savior: Ultimately with R.C. Sproul

SPROUL: There is no word more common to the 
Christian than the word savior. And how often  

we can use that term without ever really thinking 
about what it means. We know that Jesus has done  

something for us, but we don’t really penetrate 
to what it is that He does for us. We haven’t  

seen the depths of the danger, the acuteness of 
the calamity that stands before the whole world.

Jesus Himself guaranteed that 
that day will certainly come,  

and it is a day upon which every 
idle word will be considered,  

and every secret thought and every 
secret deed will be openly made manifest.

That terrifies me to think that every sin in my 
life will be made public knowledge. Who could  

stand such a thing? That would be calamitous 
for me. It would be calamitous for you. I need  

somebody to rescue me. I need somebody to save 
me. And that’s what God has given us: a Savior.
