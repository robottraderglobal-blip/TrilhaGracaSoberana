Now, when he begins to develop his arguments

Whatever exists in this world exists because

Because God is not nothing, God is something.

it's manifestly impossible for something to

to create itself, it would have to be before

not be at the same time and in the same relationship.
So, to argue that something comes into being

out of nothing by its own power is to jump
into a sea of pure absurdity.
