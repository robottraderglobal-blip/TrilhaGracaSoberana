Father, don’t allow us to give ourselves 
again to sin that it might have dominion  

over us. By the power of Thy Spirit 
help us to preserve our liberty,  

to walk in that liberty by which we have been 
freed by the Holy Spirit, and by Thy grace help  

us to make the seeking of righteousness the main 
business of our lives, not to merit salvation,  

which we couldn’t possibly do, but to show 
forth our praise and our gratitude to you. Amen.
