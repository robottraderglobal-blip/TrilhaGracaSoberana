lashed out against Jews for various reasons in  

the sixteenth century, and in a manner that was 
not really all that unusual in the polemics of  

that day. But earlier on in his ministry Martin 
Luther wrote a magnificent essay on the debt that  

the church of Christ owes to the Jews, in which in 
this great essay Luther pointed out the biblical  

principle Salvation is of the Jews. And in that 
essay, which is often overlooked in the debate,  

people fail to hear Luther saying we have
