# New Bible Curriculum for Children: Growing in God’s Word

a quiet crisis is unfolding in the church fewer children are learning the Bible young people are disengaging biblical literacy is in decline as Christians how can we faithfully raise the next generation in the discipline and instruction of the Lord children need to be taught the truth of God's word they need faithful guidance to know him trust him and walk in his ways we must be intentional in discipling them and it starts with a firm foundation in scripture that's why Ligamier created growing in God's word growing in God's word is a new curriculum designed to help churches guide elementary age children to an overview of the Bible in a year built on the unique authority of scripture every lesson reinforces the truths of reformed theology and the Bible's unifying covenantal framework children will hear more than Bible stories they will discover their place in God's story of salvation in and through Jesus Christ by God's grace we can equip the next generation with confidence and children can develop a firm foundation in God's word let's teach them together let's grow in God's word
