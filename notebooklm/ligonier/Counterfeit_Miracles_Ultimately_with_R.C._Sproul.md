# Counterfeit Miracles: Ultimately with R.C. Sproul

SPROUL: One of the discussions I get in all 
the time with people is whether Satan can  

actually perform miracles. And I say no, 
no, no. Satan never performed a miracle  

in his life. “But doesn’t the Bible warn us 
against the signs that Satan will perform,  

deceiving, if possible, even the elect?” 
But how are those signs described?  

Lying signs and wonders. That doesn’t mean that 
they are true miracles performed in the service of  

satanic purposes. No, they are not true signs used 
for a bad reason. They are false signs, tricks.
