# Where Is God When I Need Him?: Questions about God with R.C. Sproul

SPROUL: I remember that when I was in college in my
senior year, I had to fill up some elective

“And He walks with me, and He talks with

I think that if I could have any experience

in the flesh, and to walk with Him, to speak
