shepherd and as under-shepherds coming  

before You and behind You, we ask that 
You remind us that we are mere servants  

and stewards carrying the mysteries of 
Christ Jesus that we want to declare. Lord,  

in a day and age where many of us struggle with 
declaring our message and who we are, we pray  

that we may die to ourselves, that only the 
name of Christ Jesus may be lifted on high,  

that you would remind us by the workings of 
the Spirit that we live under authority and  

accountability, that indeed our lives will 
be characterized by our dependence upon You.

Thank you for loving my brothers and sisters that 
are here and pastors who labor so faithfully in  

their local churches. Though we do not know 
the hearts of both burden and successes,  

You know them. So, Lord, would you go before them 
and allow them to see with eyes wide open by the  

Spirit, how faithful and present You are in their 
lives. May they until the day the Lord returns,  

remain faithful to You as we lift up the 
name of Christ Jesus on high. Thank You  

for this time where we can encourage and 
sharpen one another and through it all,  

may Your name be honored and glorified. 
For we pray all these things in the  

matchless and loving name of Your 
Son, Christ Jesus, our Lord. Amen.
