# God Will Keep His Promises: Ultimately with R.C. Sproul

SPROUL: The God that we worship has the power to  

fulfill every promise that 
He has made to His people.

Now, everybody in this room has had the 
experience of listening to somebody make  

idle boasts. “I can do this, and I can do 
this. Anything you can do, I can do better,”  

and so on. And we’ve seen people when the crunch 
comes not have the ability to deliver. And we  

take that human dimension of idle boasting 
and project it on God Himself, as if God  

either were a liar or an impotent weakling. 
As if God were sitting in His heaven saying,  

“I’m trying to bring about the redemption that 
I have promised you. I’m trying as hard as I  

can. Please be patient with Me, but I have to 
go through another aerobics program before I  

can get My arms strong enough to pull off all 
of those things that I have said I would do.”

But the God that we worship, the God that we 
believe, has the power. He has the strength to  

do what He will do. He has the power to fulfill 
every promise that He has made to His people.  

And that’s where our faith fails us, I think, 
and that’s because we don’t know who God is.
