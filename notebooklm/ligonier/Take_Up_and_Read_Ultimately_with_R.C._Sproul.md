# Take Up and Read: Ultimately with R.C. Sproul

SPROUL: I’m amazed at the nuances 
of Scripture and how in all these  

tiny details, they fit together with 
so much symmetry. I don’t ask you to  

shut off your mind when you read the 
Scripture; I ask you to turn it on.

If you are struggling with the question of the 
authority of the Bible, the trustworthiness of  

the Bible, the truthfulness of the 
Bible, is the Bible the Word of God,  

I would give to you the same advice that 
Augustine heard centuries ago: take up and read.

Take up and read. Immerse yourselves in 
the Scripture. Think deeply about what  

the Scripture is saying to you and see if you do 
not hear the ring of truth. See if you do not see  

the consent of the Bible with all of its parts 
fitting together in an incredibly detailed way.  

I’m amazed at the nuances of Scripture 
and how in all these tiny details,  

they fit together with so much symmetry, 
intricacy woven together in the symmetry.

Again, I don’t ask you to shut off your mind when 
you read the Scripture; I ask you to turn it on.  

There’s no time that we ought to be more alert 
or more diligent in our study than when we  

are reading the Scriptures. You have nothing to 
fear from a deep, deep study of the Word of God.
