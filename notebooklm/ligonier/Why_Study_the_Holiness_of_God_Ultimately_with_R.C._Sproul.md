# Why Study the Holiness of God?: Ultimately with R.C. Sproul

SPROUL: I wish I understood the holiness 
of God. I understand it a little bit,  

just a fraction. I study God’s holiness not 
because I’m holy but because I’m not holy,  

and I know it. It’s not enough simply to say, 
“I believe in God, some amorphous, mysterious,  

cloudy Spirit up in the sky.” But the God 
we believe in is personal and the God that  

we believe in is moral. He is ethical. 
He is holy. And He gives commandments  

to His people. And He gets exercised 
about whether we obey those commands.
