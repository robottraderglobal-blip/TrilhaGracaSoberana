# Are We Depraved?: Ultimately with R.C. Sproul

SPROUL: How do you respond to the concept total 
depravity? Emotionally now—I’m not asking you for  

your intellectual evaluation or analysis of it. 
But is there a visceral reaction, a gut reaction  

to this concept as you hear it? Is this an idea 
that you want to distance yourself from because  

it’s so pessimistic, so gloomy, and so denigrating 
of our humanity? Or do you think there is  

something to this concept of total depravity that 
is important for the Christian to understand? I  

hope that you answered it the second way, because 
part of the problem is we have lost any sense of  

the divine standard, any sense of the law of God, 
which is the mirror that tells us our moral state.
